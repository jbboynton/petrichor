We are Land of Fires.

Land of Fires is an up-and-coming instrumental rock band from Providence, RI. Originally formed in 2013, Land of Fires continues to evolve and change with each passing year. With influences ranging from Explosions in the Sky and Caspian, to Animals as Leaders and Periphery, to dredg and The Dear Hunter, Land of Fires offers a fresh look at the post rock landscape.

The self-titled EP Land of Fires was released at the end of December, 2014. Since then, Land of Fires has performed around Rhode Island. As of the beginning of 2017, the band began work on their second EP.
